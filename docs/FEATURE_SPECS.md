# EcoGlobe - Feature Specifications

## Public Portal (350 hours)

| Component | Feature | Effort | Hours |
|-----------|---------|--------|-------|
| Public Website | Homepage, marketplace overview, static pages, CTA to sign up | Medium | 160 |
| Stock Visibility | Public browse/view of available stock and summary feedstock data | Medium | 110 |
| Registration Entry | User signup entry, lead capture, validation and onboarding handoff | Low | 80 |

## Seller Portal (790 hours)

| Component | Feature | Effort | Hours |
|-----------|---------|--------|-------|
| Onboarding | Seller registration, company profile, verification workflow and approval states | High | 120 |
| Listing Management | Create, edit, publish and manage feedstock listings with specs and attachments | High | 190 |
| Sales Management | View inquiries, orders, sales pipeline and transaction progression | High | 180 |
| Accounting | Seller statements, payout visibility, invoices and transaction summaries | Medium | 120 |
| Reports | Seller reports for listings, transactions, revenue and activity | Medium | 110 |
| Notifications | Seller alerts for approvals, inquiries, orders and delivery updates | Low | 40 |
| Settings | Company settings, user preferences and account controls | Low | 30 |

## Buyer Portal (780 hours)

| Component | Feature | Effort | Hours |
|-----------|---------|--------|-------|
| Onboarding | Buyer registration, company setup, approval and profile management | Medium | 80 |
| Marketplace Setup | Search, filters, map/listing views, saved preferences and discovery tools | High | 180 |
| Order Pickup | Pickup scheduling, coordination points and pickup status workflow | Medium | 80 |
| Order Delivery | Delivery tracking, confirmation and proof-of-delivery workflow | Medium | 80 |
| Order Management | Inquiries, quotes/orders, statuses, history and actions | High | 180 |
| Accounting | Buyer invoices, payment records, account statements and summaries | Medium | 90 |
| Company Setup | Business profile, addresses, contacts and operational preferences | Low | 40 |
| Notifications | Buyer alerts for listing changes, orders, pickup and delivery | Low | 30 |
| Settings | User preferences, saved filters and account controls | Low | 20 |

## Admin Portal (970 hours)

| Component | Feature | Effort | Hours |
|-----------|---------|--------|-------|
| Sales | Admin oversight of transactions, pipeline, exceptions and controls | High | 210 |
| Listings | Moderation and management of marketplace listings and content quality | High | 180 |
| Sellers | View, verify and manage seller profiles, approvals and activity along with products and sales | Medium | 130 |
| Buyers | View and manage buyer profiles, approvals and account details with order details | Medium | 120 |
| Accounting | Admin finance views, covering transactions with escrow oversight and reconciliations | High | 140 |
| Reports | Platform-wide reports, KPI dashboards and export controls | Medium | 140 |
| Settings | Global platform settings, roles, permissions and controls | Medium | 20 |
| Notifications | Notification templates, trigger rules and communication oversight | Medium | 30 |

## Shared Core (600 hours)

| Component | Feature | Effort | Hours |
|-----------|---------|--------|-------|
| Authentication & RBAC | Login, password reset, role-based access and secure session handling | High | 120 |
| Document Management | Upload, storage and retrieval of verification and listing documents | Medium | 80 |
| Search Engine | Search and filter engine for listings, location and key attributes | High | 80 |
| Workflow | Shared status transitions, approvals and rule-driven actions | High | 80 |
| Reporting | Reusable reporting/export services across all portals | Medium | 100 |
| Notification | Central email and in-app notification services and templates | Medium | 80 |
| Audit & Security | Audit logs, permission matrix, security hardening and traceability | Medium | 60 |
